# 🏪 Retail Business Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Retail Business Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to provide comprehensive insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product categories, and regional trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify growth opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor overall retail business performance.
* Analyze sales and profit across regions and product categories.
* Identify high-value customer segments.
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior.
* Discover top-performing customers and regions.
* Support strategic business decisions using data-driven insights.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Regional Performance

* West region generated the highest sales among all regions.
* Ontario and Prairie were among the strongest-performing regions.
* Regional performance analysis highlights significant revenue concentration in key areas.

### Product Performance

* Technology was the highest-performing category by sales.
* Furniture ranked second in overall sales contribution.
* Office Supplies generated the largest number of orders.

### Customer Analysis

* Corporate customers generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Corporate segment also accounted for the highest quantity sold and order volume.
* Top customers contributed a significant share of total business revenue.

### Sales Trends

* Monthly sales remained relatively stable throughout the year.
* Strong sales performance was observed during the beginning and end of the year.
